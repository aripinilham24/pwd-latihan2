COFFEE ESPRESSO

Espresso is a strong, concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. Known for its bold flavor and rich crema, espresso serves as the base for many popular coffee drinks like cappuccinos and lattes. Its small serving size packs a punch, offering an intense caffeine boost with each sip.

COFFEE CAPPUCINO

A classic Italian favorite, cappuccino is a delightful blend of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Its creamy texture and balanced flavor make it a comforting choice for coffee lovers. Often enjoyed with a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon, it's perfect for those who appreciate a harmonious mix of strong coffee and smooth, frothy milk.

COFFEE LATTE

The latte is a smooth, milky coffee drink that combines a shot of espresso with a generous amount of steamed milk and a light layer of foam. With its subtle coffee flavor and creamy texture, it's ideal for those who prefer a less intense coffee experience. Lattes can also be flavored with syrups like vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut, adding a touch of sweetness to the beverage.
